We are a non-profit institution whose objective is to publicize, promote and encourage the practice and teaching of mountain sports responsibly and safely. MEC is not just a school where you go to learn how to climb mountains. We always seek to go further.As we believe it is possible to build a better world through the sport we love so much, we develop projects that embody our opinion, such as our social actions – Climbing for the Visually Impaired and Adopt a Mountain.

And our courses, designed ​both for those who know nothing about the sport, and for those who already climb and are looking forward to new challenges. MEC is the realization of the dream of a group of mountaineers who have been carrying out activities in mountains since 1995, people who are passionate about what they do, sharing knowledge and life in the outdoors. Every year we change many people's lives.
